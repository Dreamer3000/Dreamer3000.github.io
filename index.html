SBU Synthetic Biology Society


Synthetic Biology is an interdisciplinery field between clasical biology and engineering, aiming to tackle many problems humanity faces in the modern world. The applications range from hardier crops to gene editing to grant immunity to the worst diseases that plague our species. This is a club focused on introducing this growing field and all its components to students in a more casual setting with presentations, demonstrations and activities. Meeting topics focus on anything from 3D printing to how animals handle low temperatures safely and reliably. Stop by to learn something new, chill out and win some free stuff!

We meet Mondays Bi-weekly, 7:00 - 7:50 p.m.
